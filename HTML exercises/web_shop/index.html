Pizza Trivia

When did Pizza first appear in the United States?

Pizza arrived in the United States in the early 20th century along with waves of Italian immigrants who settled primarily in the large cities of the Northeast. The first pizzeria in the U.S., Lombardi's,opened in New York City's Little Italy in 1905, producing a Neapolitan-style pizza. (Source: Wikipedia)
